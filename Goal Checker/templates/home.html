Goal Summary

Date Started: February 2023

Expected Date to Achieve: December 2027

Goal: Become a Computer Vision Engineer

Objective: To become proficient in designing, developing, and deploying computer vision solutions for real-world applications.

Skill Level: Aim to reach an expert level, capable of both independent and collaborative project work.

Breakdown of Goals

  • Programming: Master Python and libraries like OpenCV, PyTorch, TensorFlow, and scikit-image.
  • Deep Learning Foundations: Gain strong understanding of CNNs, transfer learning, and model optimization.
  • Computer Vision Techniques: Master object detection, segmentation, and other vision-related tasks.
  • Tools & Frameworks: Proficiency in TensorFlow, PyTorch Lightning, OpenCV, etc.
  • Research & Development: Stay up-to-date with advancements through papers and conferences (CVPR, ICCV).

Practical Experience

Build projects focusing on real-world applications such as:

  • Autonomous Vehicles
  • Facial Recognition
  • Medical Image Analysis
  • Agriculture and Environmental Monitoring

Soft Skills

  • Improve problem-solving and critical thinking.
  • Enhance communication for presenting technical ideas.
  • Collaborate effectively in interdisciplinary teams.
